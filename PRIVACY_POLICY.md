# 口袋机位（PocketCam）隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 2 日

口袋机位（PocketCam）是一款将手机作为局域网摄像头使用的工具。我们重视您的隐私，并尽量以简单、透明的方式说明 App 如何处理信息。

## 我们不会收集或上传您的数据

口袋机位不会将视频、图像、连接信息或个人信息上传至开发者服务器。App 不包含广告、用户追踪或第三方分析 SDK，也不会向第三方出售或共享您的数据。

## 相机与本地网络

口袋机位需要使用相机权限来采集视频画面，并需要使用本地网络权限在您当前连接的局域网内提供浏览器控制页、MJPEG 视频流和 RTSP 视频流。

视频流仅在您的设备与局域网内主动连接的客户端之间传输。请仅在您信任的局域网内使用本 App，并设置不易猜测的连接用户名和密码。

## 本地保存的信息

您在 App 中设置的连接用户名和密码会保存在设备本地，用于局域网访问认证。局域网客户端的当前连接与连接历史仅用于在 App 运行期间展示连接状态，不会上传至开发者服务器。

## 数据保留与删除

由于我们不会收集或上传您的数据，因此开发者服务器不会保留任何用户数据。您可以通过删除 App 清除保存在设备本地的设置。

## 儿童隐私

口袋机位不会主动收集任何用户（包括儿童）的个人信息。

## 隐私政策更新

如本隐私政策发生变化，我们会在此页面更新内容和生效日期。

# PocketCam Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

PocketCam is a utility that turns your phone into a camera accessible on your local network. We value your privacy and explain below how the app handles information.

## We Do Not Collect or Upload Your Data

PocketCam does not upload video, images, connection information, or personal information to developer-operated servers. The app does not include advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics SDKs. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## Camera and Local Network Access

PocketCam requires camera access to capture video and local network access to provide a browser control page, an MJPEG video stream, and an RTSP video stream on your current local network.

Video streams are transmitted only between your device and clients that actively connect through your local network. Please use the app only on networks you trust and set a connection username and password that are difficult to guess.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The connection username and password configured in the app are stored locally on your device and used for local network access authentication. Current and historical local network connections are shown only while the app is running and are not uploaded to developer-operated servers.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not collect or upload your data, no user data is retained on developer-operated servers. You can remove locally stored settings by deleting the app.

## Children's Privacy

PocketCam does not knowingly collect personal information from any user, including children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this privacy policy changes, we will update this page and its effective date.
